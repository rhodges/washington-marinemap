Multi-Objective Analysis

The multi-objective tradeoffs report compares the potential valuation of your shapes for both Wind Energy and Conservation.

The results are displayed such that shapes that are valuated higher for Conservation will be further along the x-axis (to the right), and those that are valuated higher for Wind Energy Potential will be further along the y-axis (toward the top). Conflict areas, those areas that have high valuations for both objectives, will be seen in the upper-right quadrant of the graph.
