Washington Marine Planner

The Washington Marine Planner is a pilot project intended to support the state's Marine Spatial Planning (MSP) process and the Pacific County Shoreline Master Plan (SMP) data and inventory characterization.

Next Steps

The Marine Planner has been designed to help you:

  • Identify Scenarios for oceanic use objectives that include Renewable Energy and Conservation

  • Design Areas (including Sites specific to the SMP process) for further analysis

  • Analyze your Areas for Tradeoffs between the conservation and energy objectives

Get started by selecting the Create New menu from the My Shapes panel, or click 'help', located above the map panel, to view a Tutorial. {% endblock panel %}
