Washington Marine Planner

The Washington Marine Planner is being developed in support of Pacific County's Shoreline Master Program.

More information on SMPs for the state of Washington can be found here.

Next Steps

The Marine Planner has been designed to help you:

  • Identify Scenarios for oceanic use objectives that include Renewable Energy and Conservation

  • Design Areas (including Sites specific to the SMP process) for further analysis, and then

  • Analyze your Areas for Tradeoffs between the conservation and energy objectives
